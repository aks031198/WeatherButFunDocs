Privacy and support for the weather poster app.

Weather But Fun turns live weather into a playful visual scene. This site hosts the app's privacy policy, support policy, and a short overview of how the app handles weather, location, widgets, local caching, and Apple-managed premium subscriptions.

Read Privacy Policy Open Support Policy

At a Glance

What the current app does

  • Requests location only when you use the app and only if you grant permission.
  • Fetches live weather and attribution from Apple Weather via WeatherKit.
  • Caches the last weather snapshot locally so the app and widget can keep working.
  • Stores visual preferences, theme state, and local subscription status needed for the app experience.
  • Offers monthly and yearly premium subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store.
  • Does not include account creation, ads, or third-party analytics in the current build.

Privacy

Policy summary

The app uses location to request nearby weather, saves the last fetched weather scene locally, and shares that cached snapshot with the widget through the app group. Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple, and the app does not currently run third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
